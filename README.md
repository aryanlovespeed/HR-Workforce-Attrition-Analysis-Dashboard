# 👥 HR Workforce & Attrition Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee workforce data to understand attrition patterns, employee demographics, salary distribution, job roles, promotions, overtime, and overall workforce performance.

The dashboard was developed using Microsoft Excel to help HR teams identify the key factors influencing employee attrition and support better workforce planning.

---

## Objective

The objective of this project was to convert employee data into actionable HR insights that can assist management in improving employee retention and organizational performance.

## Business Analysis

The dashboard analyzes:

- Employee Attrition by Department
- Attrition by Job Role
- Attrition by Gender
- Attrition by Education
- Attrition by Age Group
- Attrition by Salary Band
- Overtime Impact
- Promotion Analysis
- Job Satisfaction
- Employee Experience

---

## Key Business Insights

Some important findings include:

- Employees working overtime experienced significantly higher attrition.
- Team Leads received higher salaries and maintained strong performance, but many still left due to delayed promotions and workload.
- Certain departments contributed disproportionately to overall attrition.
- Employee experience and promotion opportunities showed a clear relationship with retention.
